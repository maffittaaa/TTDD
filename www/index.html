How to Play:

At the beginning of the game, each player starts with the same 3 characters!

The players can unlock more characters as they progress in the game, gaining XP after each battle.

In total there are 10 different characters with different abilities and types: 5 close-range characters and 5 long-range characters.

Each player has access to a deck of 10 cards with different abilities in every match.
