How to Play:

At the beginning of the game, each player starts with the same 3 characters!

As the players play more games, they will gain XP (more XP for winning battles, less for losing battles).

The XP that the players gain, makes them level up. Leveling up is important to unlock better characters and to battle against stronger enemies.

There are 5 positions in which the characters can be placed, 3 in front for the close-range characters, and 2 in the back for the long-range characters.

Close-range characters can only attack the other close-range characters.

Long-range characters can attack every character.

Each player, during the game, will have access to a deck of cards where there will be 10 cards, with different uses.

The cards can only be picked up one at a time and every 2 turns. The players, after picking the card, can choose to play it or to store it.
